    About Us

    What We are and How we grow!


    Makesol Dresses, established in 1991, is a renowned name in women’s ethnic wear, offering an extensive range of stylish and high-quality garments. From kurtis, co-ord sets, and sharara suits to midi dresses and Afghani pant sets, the brand blends traditional craftsmanship with contemporary designs. Catering to sizes M to 5XL, their products are crafted using premium fabrics like cotton and rayon, ensuring both comfort and durability.

    Operating under the creative label “Makesol,” Dresses is committed to delivering elegant designs that reflect the rich heritage of Indian textiles while embracing modern trends. With over three decades of expertise, they have built a reputation for exceptional quality and customer satisfaction, making them a prominent choice in Ahmedabad’s thriving textile market.
