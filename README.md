
# Makesol Fashion 👗

A modern and elegant fashion website showcasing premium ethnic wear collections by **Makesol Dresses**. The site highlights quality, sustainability, and customer-first values, giving users a seamless browsing experience.

🌐 **Live Demo:** [https://makesolfasion.netlify.app/](https://makesolfasion.netlify.app/)

---

## ✨ Features

* 🎨 **Responsive Design** – Works across desktop, tablet, and mobile
* 🖼 **Image Carousel & Galleries** – Showcasing standout collections
* 📖 **About Us Section** – Brand story and growth
* ✅ **Core Values Section** – Quality assurance, sustainability, customer satisfaction
* 📞 **Contact Integration** – WhatsApp, email, and phone support
* ⚡ **Deployed on Netlify** – Fast and reliable hosting

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

* **HTML5**
* **CSS3**
* **JavaScript (Vanilla)**
* **Bootstrap** (for layout & components)
* **Netlify** (for deployment & hosting)

---
